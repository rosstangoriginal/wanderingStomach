France

I have made two journeys to Paris and it is my favourite city in the world.


The French are known for their culinary traditions and they did not disappoint.


The standout dish for me is cassoulet. It is a hearty stew from the South-West of France containing duck confit, beans, and sausage. When I first tried the dish, I finished the entire bowl much to the astonishment of my waiter.


I found that in Paris, even the simplest meal is of the utmost quality. Even a simplest baguette sandwich at the train station would give most other sandwiches a run for their money.
