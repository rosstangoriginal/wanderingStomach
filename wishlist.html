Wishlist

The world has a wealth of diversity and its cuisines are no exception. There are so many interesting places to visit but a few destinations top the list for me.


Japan is definitely at the top of the list for me. I enjoy many kinds of Japanese food, from sushi to ramen, from tonkatsu to teppanyaki. I have enjoyed following YouTubers who showcase the different foods of Japan. This includes the bounty of amazing foods you can enjoy at Japanese convenience stores. I wouldn't know how much time to spend in Japan because there are so many foods to try and so many interesting places to discover.


Another top destination is South Korea. Thankfully both it and Japan could be visited within the same trip due to their proximity. I do not know a lot about South Korea and where to visit but my wife and I know many International Students who have moved back to South Korea after studying in Canada. On top of visiting old friends, there's also the food. While I have not been exposed to all of the wonderful cuisine of Korea, I have very much so enjoyed Korean BBQ, bibimbap, and LA galbi. I also love the small side dishes called banchan, that are served with your meal. My favourite is the sweet fried potatoes.
